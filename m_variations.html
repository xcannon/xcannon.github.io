Wolfgang A. Mozart

Themes & Variations

Mozart enjoyed “riffing” on others’ melodies, & his own as well.  Here are several of his classics, including the instantly recognizable French folk song, Ah! vous dirai-je, maman, on which he supplies no fewer than twelve variations.   The first selection, aka William of Orange, was composed at the age of 10.
