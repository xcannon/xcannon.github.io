* The piece in A minor is known far & wide as the 8th sonata; however, it has long since been determined that the one in D major was composed before it—in fact right after the one in C major, in 1777.  I present them numbered in the order they were written, having no better scheme to go by.  And just FYI, the first 5 of these were written basically in a bunch, when Wolfgang was 18 years of age.
