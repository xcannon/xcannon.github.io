Johann Sebastian Bach

Capriccio on the Departure of his Most Beloved Brother

1704 – harpsichord

Arioso 

Andante 

Adagissimo 

Aria di Postiglione 

Fuga 

This is the only one for which I’ve provided any info; there are quaint little descriptions of what each movement is about, as follows:

  • Arioso: Adagio – Is a wheedling by friends in order to keep him from his journey
  • Andante – Is a setting-forth of various casualties that could befall him abroad
  • Adagissimo – Is a general lamento by his friends
      — Here the friends come, seeing after all that it cannot be otherwise, and bid farewell
  • Aria di Postiglione: Adagio poco – Postilion’s Air
  • Fuga – Fugue in imitation of the postilion’s horn
