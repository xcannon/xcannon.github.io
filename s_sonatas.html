Domenico Scarlatti

Eight Sonatas

These are presented in no special order, & range from under 1 minute to about 7 & a half.  They are from a book I checked out at the library; the dynamics were someone else’s impressions, which I perhaps followed a bit too well.  (The same book contained some of the Clementi & Dussek sonatinas also featured here.)  They were written for the harpsichord, but sound just great on piano.  Note that each & every one is in proper sonata form.

#1 in E - Capriccio 

#2 in Dm - Gavotta 

#3 in B♭ - Menuetto 

#4 in C 

#5 in Fm 

#6 in G 

#7 in Dm - Pastorale 

#8 in D - Balletto
