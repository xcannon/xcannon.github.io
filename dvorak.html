Antonín Dvořák

1841 – 1904

Dvořák is probably Bohemia’s best-known composer.  (Bohemia, for those who still don’t know, was once the name of the country that is now the Czech Republic.)  His most famous work is his 9th Symphony, aka the “New World” symphony.

This album is the entire content—in order—of a book of selections from his compositions for piano.
