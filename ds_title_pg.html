Sophia Dussek

Three Harp Sonatas – Title page

Occasionally the sheet music downloads are in original form (they can be fairly taxing to read), complete with title page.  Some of these are quite amusing &/or charming.  This is the text of page 1 of Sophia’s harp sonatas, Op. 2.  It is also, of course, floridly illustrated.
