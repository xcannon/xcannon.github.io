The insipid saga of the Yahoo WebPlayer



After creating & providing for web developers a highly useful & convenient tool, Yahoo arbitrarily—& with no explanation—stopped making the player available in July 2013, pissing off thousands of hapless web authors whose pages suddenly started doing nothing.  I can’t believe how stupid they are.  That’s the whole story, really; the rest is just blogs, boards & fora full of endless bitching.

The happy ending, for me anyway, is that someone copped the script (it may be that Yahoo allowed it) & I found it, d/l’ed it, put it in my home dir, changed the link to the local copy, & it works even better!  In case anyone’s looking for it, here is where I got it:
