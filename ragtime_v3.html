Ragtime

Vol. 3

1906 – 1909

I’ve attempted to present these roughly in the order they were published, but Artie Matthews’ Pastime Rags were written long before they saw print, sometime in his St. Louis years—190-something; by the time they were published he had left ragtime behind & founded a conservatory in Cincinnati ... so I just bunched them up here.  They are outstandingly original, even among all these other very creative works.
