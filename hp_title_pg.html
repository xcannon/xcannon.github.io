Henry Purcell

Harpsichord Lessons – Title page

Yes: these little ditties were intended as lessons for beginning players.  There is even an introduction to the representational scheme of music in just 5 pages.


A CHOICE COLLECTION
of
Leſsons for the Harpſichord or Spinnet
Compoſed by ye late M.r Henry Purcell Organist of his
Majesties Chappel Royal, & of St. Peters Weſtminster
The third Edition with Additions & Instructions for beginers
Printed on Copper Plates for M.rs Frances Purcell Executrix
of the Author, and are to be sold at her houſe in Great
Deans Yard Weſtminster
