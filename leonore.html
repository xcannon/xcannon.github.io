Ludwig van Beethoven

Overture – Leonore No. 3

1806 – full orchestra

This is Beethoven’s 3rd version (of 4) of the overture to his only opera, which he named Leonore, but—inexplicably—everyone else calls Fidelio.  It is by far the most elaborate thing on the site, surpassing even my own more layered works.  No surprise really: it’s a full orchestral score with 15 instrumental parts, many of those with inner harmonies.  It’s encoded at a higher bitrate & goes almost 15 minutes, so it’s a big file: at 20+ M the largest one here (yet not quite the longest; that’d be pt. 3 of Mozart’s 6th sonata).
