Xeno’s Mighty Music Pages


At long last!  My extensive—& continually growing—collection of home-sequenced music is publicly available: everyone’s to enjoy for absolutely free!

I’ve edited tags on these, so they’ll collect into albums when you put ’em on your i-pod or wherever.  To download any tune, you should just be able to click on its text link; but if that doesn’t work, consult download help.

The operation of the Yahoo player is pretty straightforward, but there can be problems, & if you encounter any, check with player help.  One thing you should know up front is that the player sometimes skips a song, if the load doesn’t happen fast enough.  So you kinda gotta keep an eye on it.

Display issues are addressed at the bottom of the helpdesk page.

To find out what’s changed since your last visit, see What’s New?
